About


Our purpose

Our goal was to build an Edinburgh landmark database-driven guessing game.Whether you are a local, an Edinburgh aficionado or you've never heard of this place before, we made sure this game is for you. To answer our questions, use your local knowledge and/or your inquisitive skills. The answer is somewhere in the maps provided - you just need to look for it!


Image by Vijt Bagh from Pexels



How did we do this?

This game is database-driven. This means that we digitised selected landmarks in a GIS and imported the resulting data into Oracle Spatial. We then created specific queries that will run when you select your answer, determining whether you guess ir right or wrong. For each question, we also designed specific database queries to retrieve and plot the data onto a folium map.
